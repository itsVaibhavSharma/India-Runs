# Senior AI Engineer — Founding Team @ Redrob AI

## About the Role

Redrob AI is building the next-generation intelligent recruiting platform. We're looking for a Senior AI Engineer (Founding Team) to lead the development of our candidate ranking, retrieval, and matching systems.

This is not a research role — we ship to production. You'll own the ML systems that serve real users from day one.

## What You'll Build

- **Production embedding and retrieval systems**: Dense + sparse hybrid search over 500K+ candidate profiles using FAISS, Weaviate, or Qdrant
- **Learning-to-rank pipelines**: NDCG/MRR-evaluated ranking models trained on implicit signals (clicks, applications, hires)
- **Semantic matching**: Sentence-transformers / BGE / E5 models fine-tuned on recruiting-specific data
- **RAG systems**: Context-aware candidate summaries and job matching explanations
- **A/B testing infrastructure**: Offline evaluation (NDCG@10, MAP, MRR) + online experiment framework

## Requirements

### Must Have
- **5-9 years** of experience in applied ML / AI engineering
- Production experience deploying **embedding-based retrieval or search** systems (FAISS, Pinecone, Weaviate, Qdrant, Milvus, OpenSearch)
- Strong **Python** skills — you write clean, testable, production-grade code
- Experience building and evaluating **ranking systems** (NDCG, MRR, MAP, A/B testing)
- Track record of shipping ML systems to **real users at scale** (not just research papers or demos)
- Experience at a **product company** (startup, scale-up, or established tech) — not services/consulting

### Nice to Have
- LLM fine-tuning (LoRA, QLoRA, PEFT) for domain adaptation
- Learning-to-rank frameworks (XGBoost ranking, LightGBM, LambdaMART, ListNet)
- HR-tech, recruiting, or marketplace domain experience
- Distributed training / large-scale inference optimization
- Open-source contributions to ML tooling

### Disqualifiers (Please do not apply if)
- Your entire ML experience is LangChain/LlamaIndex wrappers with no underlying ML understanding
- You haven't written production-deployed ML code in 18+ months (purely management/architecture roles)
- Your background is 100% research with no deployment experience
- All your career has been at IT services/consulting firms (TCS, Infosys, Wipro, Cognizant, Capgemini, Accenture, HCL, Tech Mahindra)
